History of Mattaquot

Mattaquot (pronounced /Ma·ta·cot/) was admitted to the Union on May 16, 1867, becoming the 39th state of the United States of America. Today, Mattaquot is the largest state in New England and though sparsely populated retains a sizeable Native American community.

Mattaquot Today

The 3rd Mattaquot Constitution came into effect on January 1, 1928 following which the State Assembly assimilated the crimes taken to be understood from Common Law at the time of the state's independence, and subsequently made law by the Assembly, codifying them into the Mattaquot State Code. The Constitution also allowed for the state government to better reorganise and distribute political power to counties and municipalities as prescribed by law, culminating the Municipalities Act and the Reorganisation of Counties Act in 1931.

Residency

To become a resident of the State of Mattaquot, you must complete the Residency Test. In order to become a resident, and complete the Residency Test, the following requirements must be met.

  • Be a Visitor in the State of Mattaquot group
  • Played and must be aware of the Hopewell County map and its surroundings
  • Must have a clean moderation record in the State of Mattaquot Discord servers and Hopewell County (this is subject to the discretion of a developer)
  • Must have an appropriate ROBLOX username, bio, and status pursuant to ROBLOX Terms of Service
  • Have been on ROBLOX for at least 30 days before starting the Residency Test
  • Have your ROBLOX "Who can see my inventory?" setting set to Friends, Users I Follow, Followers or Everyone
  • Learn More: State of Mattaquot Residency Test
